About StayEase

StayEase sits where the city skyline meets the open sea. Born from a love of modern design and relaxed coastal living, our hotel blends sleek, contemporary comfort with the calm of waterfront views.

Our Story

Founded with a simple idea — that travelers shouldn't have to choose between the energy of the city and the serenity of the sea — StayEase was built to offer both, side by side.

Our Promise

Every room, every service, and every detail is designed around ease: easy comfort, easy access to the city, and an easy escape to the water's edge.
